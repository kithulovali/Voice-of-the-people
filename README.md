# Voice of the People

**Voice of the People** is a computerized voting system based on physical voting principles.

## Physical Voting Workflow

| Diagram | Legend |
|--------|--------|
| <img src="https://github.com/Bucosa-IT/Voice-of-the-people/blob/backend/static/VOICE_OF_THE_PEOPLE.drawio.png" width="500" height="500"> | **Step 1:** The voting public comes prepared to vote for their favorite candidate. <br><br> **Step 2:** Police officers verify each voter's identity details. <br><br> **Step 3:** The voter picks a voting bulletin and votes for their preferred candidate. <br><br> **Step 4:** The voting bulletin is submitted into the voting basket (ballot box). <br><br> **Step 5:** Reverification of the voter is done before the identity card is returned. |
